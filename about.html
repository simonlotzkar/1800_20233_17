About

Find out if the icecream machine is working!

The Problem

Have you ever wished you knew ahead of time whether a McDonalds location had its icecream available, before you arrived?

The Solution

McWorking helps you answer this very question by providing a user-driven tracking app that targets this very issue. When you find yourself heading over to McDonalds for icecream, simply check the location on here to see if anyone has reported the machine's status recently!

Giving Back

To repay the favour, after visiting please submit an update to let others know. Doing so not only earns you real life karma, but also will be rewarded with in-app rewards.
