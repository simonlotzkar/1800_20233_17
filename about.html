About

Find out if the icecream machine is working!

Introducing McWorking - your solution to the notorious McDonald's ice cream machine woes!

The Problem

Have you ever wished you knew ahead of time whether a McDonalds location had its icecream available, before you arrived?

Our Solution

McWorking is your go-to app when that craving for McDonalds ice cream strikes. Wondering if your nearest McDonalds ice cream machine is up and running? Simply check the machine's status at your preferred location on McWorking. Our app relies on real-time updates from users like you, ensuring you know whether it's "working" or "broken" before you make the trip.

Giving Back

But wait, there's more! We value your contributions. Every time you update or report the status of a McDonalds ice cream machine, you earn valuable in-app rewards as our way of saying thank you.

No more disappointments at the drive-throughs, use McWorking and enjoy your ice cream without the hassle!

With this app, currently you can do the following:
  • As a user, create an account and log in or log out.
  • View the status of the icecream machine of any McDonalds location that has been updated by another user.
  • View the 3 closest McDonalds restaurants to your location on the home page.
  • View up to the 50 closest McDonalds restaurants on the catalog page.
  • Filter by address, city, or postal code on the catalog page.
  • Submit updates every 60 seconds to any location provided you are signed in.
  • View a restaurant's details including its update log that shows the personalized profile of each submitter.
  • Vote on each user's update to tell others whether they were accurate or not.
  • Customize your profile by choosing an avatar and banner to display on your posts.
  • Change your username and email at any time.
  • Send a reset password email from the profile page.
  • Unlock achievements by participating in the app.
  • As an admin, delete any update.
  • As a user, delete your own updates.
  • As an admin, submit new restaurants.
